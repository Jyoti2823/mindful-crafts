Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups are the kits suitable for? +
We have kits for all ages! Our children's kits are designed for ages 5–15, with age-appropriate complexity and safety. We also offer kits specifically for adults and working professionals looking for mindful creative activities.
How long does shipping take? +
We ship across India within 3–5 business days. Metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, and Chennai typically receive orders in 2–3 days. We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹999.
Are the materials safe for children? +
Absolutely! All our materials are certified non-toxic, child-safe, and eco-friendly. We comply with BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) safety standards and have our products independently tested.
Can I return or exchange a product? +
Yes! We offer a 30-day happiness guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll arrange a return, exchange, or full refund — no questions asked.
Do you offer subscription boxes? +
Yes! Our monthly subscription box delivers a new curated craft kit to your door every month. Subscribers get 20% off every box, free shipping, and early access to new products. Coming soon — sign up to our newsletter to be notified!
How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our support team.
