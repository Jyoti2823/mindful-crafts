Our Story

We started Mindful Crafts with one simple belief: that every family deserves more moments of screen-free joy.

🌸
Why We Started

A Brand Born from a Real Problem

"I noticed my 6-year-old reaching for a tablet before she even said good morning. That's when I knew something had to change."

That moment — experienced by our founder Jyoti in 2023 — sparked the idea for Mindful Crafts. After spending months researching child development, mindfulness, and the science of creativity, she built the first prototype kit at her kitchen table.

The response from family and friends was overwhelming. Within weeks, neighbours were asking where to buy the kits. What started as a personal solution became a movement.

Today, Mindful Crafts has helped over 12,000 families across India reclaim their evenings, rediscover creativity, and build deeper connections — one craft kit at a time.
