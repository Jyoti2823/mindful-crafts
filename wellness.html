Expert Tips

Screen Time Reduction Tips

🌅

Create Tech-Free Times

Designate specific hours as device-free — meals, the hour before bed, and first thing in the morning. Consistency is key.

🧺

The Phone Basket Method

Place a basket at the front door. Everyone — including parents — puts their phone in it when arriving home. Simple and effective.

🏆

Earn Screen Time

Instead of restricting screens, have children earn screen time by completing real-world activities like crafts, reading, or chores.

📦

The Boredom Box

Create a box filled with activity cards, small craft materials, and games. When kids say they're bored, direct them to the box.

Mindfulness Exercises

Simple Daily Practices

Gentle exercises that take less than 10 minutes and can be done anywhere.

🌬️

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Repeat 3 times. Instantly calms the nervous system.

⏱ 3 minutes
🙏

Gratitude Journaling

Write 3 things you're grateful for every morning. A simple practice that rewires the brain toward positivity.

⏱ 5 minutes
🌳

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Notice 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. An instant anxiety buster.

⏱ 5 minutes
